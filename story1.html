The Crypto Chronicles of Shamiul Alom Sagor

Also known as Roger in the digital realm

Chapter 1: The Foundation

My journey begins with a solid foundation in technology. I completed my diploma in Computer Science and Engineering, with plans to further my education with a BSc in Computer Science. Along the way, I honed my skills in graphic design, particularly with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Chapter 2: Entering the Crypto World

In 2021, I took my first steps into the exciting world of cryptocurrency. Starting as an investor, I quickly became fascinated by the potential of blockchain technology and the opportunities it presented.

Chapter 3: Forming the Dream Team

As 2022 dawned, I joined forces with two like-minded friends. Together, we embarked on an ambitious journey to create our own crypto token projects. Our roles were clear: I took on the mantle of developer, while my friends handled marketing and social media respectively.

Chapter 4: The Launch Pad

Our team hit the ground running, conceptualizing and launching 10 to 30 project ideas. We navigated the complexities of stealth launches and presales, learning and adapting with each new venture. My role involved diving deep into smart contract development, often learning on the fly and adapting code from established projects.

Chapter 5: Riding the Wave of Success

Our hard work paid off. We successfully raised an estimated 200-300 BNB on the BSC chain and 70-90 ETH on the Ethereum chain. Our projects on various EVM chains collectively raised around $10,000 USD. It was a thrilling time of growth and achievement.

Chapter 6: The Challenges of Success

With success came new challenges. As our projects grew, we found ourselves struggling to keep up with the demands of maintaining and developing them further. Personal commitments and the lure of our newfound success led to a decrease in our focus and productivity.

Chapter 7: Hard Choices

Facing the reality of our situation, we made the difficult decision to wind down our projects. We communicated openly with our communities, setting end dates for each project and removing liquidity as planned. It was a tough but necessary step.

Chapter 8: A Period of Reflection

In the aftermath, our team took time to reflect. We realized the importance of continuous learning and the need to focus on specific skills. This period of introspection lasted over a year, during which we each pursued our own paths.

Chapter 9: Looking to the Future

Now, as I stand at this new crossroads, I'm filled with determination. I'm exploring ways to leverage my experiences, focusing on developing my skills in coding and Web3 technologies. The idea of creating educational content on YouTube excites me, as does the possibility of engaging in freelance work.

Chapter 10: Balancing Act

As a married man with a 3.5-year-old son, I'm acutely aware of my responsibilities. My goal is to build a sustainable income of around $400 USD per month to support my family while pursuing my passions in technology and cryptocurrency.

The Journey Continues...

This is not the end, but a new beginning. Armed with experience, lessons learned, and a burning desire to grow, I'm ready to write the next chapters of my story in the ever-evolving world of technology and cryptocurrency.
